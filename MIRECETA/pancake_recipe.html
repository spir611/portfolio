The Ultimate Pancake Recipe...

Drama is very important in life: You have to come on with a bang. You never want to go out with a whimper. Everything can have drama if it's done right. Even a pancake.

My Secret Recipe :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 egg

Preparation...

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar and salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted buter and egg and mix together until smooth
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter onto the griddle for each pancake,
  5. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry for about 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter
