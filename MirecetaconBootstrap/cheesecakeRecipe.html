Cheesecake Recipe

"Cheesecake is the ultimate comfort food."

Unknown

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/3 heavy whipping cream

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9 inch springform pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, 1 1/4 cups sugar, and vanilla until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time. Stir in heavy cream. Pour mixture into prepared crust.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 70 minutes. Let cheesecake cool, then refrigerate overnight.
